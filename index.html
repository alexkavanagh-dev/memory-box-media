Tapes & Photos deteriorate over time, losing their colour and quality...
We can digitize and protect them before it's too late!

Tape to Digital Transfer

We can digitize any consumer format of video tape. With our multi-view, video bundle package, you will recieve your videos on custom printed DVD's presented in a high quality metal case for added protection, plus a password protected digital copy which can be streamed directly to your tablet, smartphone or smart TV.

€15 Per Tape

CUSTOM PHOTO REPAIR

We can remove damages & add vibrancy to old photos. Our photoshop expert can repair your old photos and even bring them to life with a hint of colour. Each photo repair project is treated on a case to case basis as each photo is unique.

€35 Per Photo

PHOTO & SLIDE SCANNING

We can digitise any consumer format of photo, including stacks of prints, slides, and even negatives. You will receive your photos on custom printed DVD's presented in a high quality metal case for added protection, plus a password-protected digital copy which will be emailed to you to share with friends and family.

€0.15 Per Photo

AUDIO TAPE/ REEL TO DIGITAL TRANSFER

We digitise cassette tapes, reel to reel and vinyl. With each tape, you will receive your recordings on custom printed CD's presented in a high quality metal case for added protection. We can also reduce background noise & enhance the sound.

€15 Per Tape
